## Key Findings

1. **Layer-2 costs are 90-95% lower than Ethereum mainnet**
   - Ethereum L1: $1.10-$5.48 per transaction
   - Leading L2s (Arbitrum, Optimism): $0.09-$0.27 per transaction
   
2. **Cheapest L2 options: $0.04-$0.09**
   - Metis Network, Loopring, zkSync, Optimism, Arbitrum
   
3. **Polygon zkEVM is more expensive than other L2s**
   - $0.19 for simple transfers
   - $2.75 for token swaps (higher than most L2s)

## Comparison to Original Claims

**Original Report Claimed:**
- Ethereum low gas: $0.28 per transaction
- Ethereum moderate: $5.00 per transaction

**Current Reality (Dec 2025):**
- Ethereum L1: $1.10 per transaction (simple transfer)
- Major L2s (Arbitrum/Optimism): $0.09 per transaction
- **Original claim was 3x higher than current Ethereum L1**
- **Original claim was 31x higher than current L2 costs**

## Impact on TCO Analysis

For a marketplace processing 100,000 transactions/month:
- **Original Ethereum claim:** $28,000/month ($0.28/tx)
- **Current Ethereum L1 reality:** $110,000/month ($1.10/tx)
- **Current L2 reality (Arbitrum/Optimism):** $9,000/month ($0.09/tx)

The original analysis understated Ethereum L1 costs but dramatically overstated L2 costs by not including them as alternatives.
