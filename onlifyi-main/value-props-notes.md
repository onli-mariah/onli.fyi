## Competitive Positioning Summary

**Onli vs. Blockchain:**
- **Onli wins on:** Cost (91-98% cheaper), Speed (1000x faster), Scalability (infinite), Legal status (balance-sheet-ready), Energy efficiency (99.99% less), Developer accessibility (no specialists needed)
- **Blockchain's only advantage:** Existing network effects and brand recognition (temporary)

**Onli vs. Traditional Databases:**
- **Onli wins on:** Uniqueness guarantee (EVD protocol), Exclusive possession (hardware-enforced), Legal property status (not just data), Embedded provenance (self-verifying), Transferability (atomic moves)
- **Database's only advantage:** Mature tooling and familiarity (addressable through Onli's simple APIs)

**Onli vs. Centralized Platforms:**
- **Onli wins on:** True ownership (not access), No custody risk (self-custody), Censorship resistance (peer-to-peer), Privacy (no platform surveillance), Portability (assets in your Vault)
- **Platform's only advantage:** User experience simplicity (Onli.You app addresses this)
