**Source**: BOSS Money (July 2025), NerdWallet (Aug 2025)

### Cost Components Breakdown

#### 1. Sending Bank Fees
- **Typical range**: $25-$50 per transaction
- **Online vs. in-person**: Online transfers often $5-$10 cheaper
- **Most visible cost**: Disclosed upfront by sending bank

#### 2. Receiving Bank Fees
- **Typical range**: $10-$20 per transaction
- **Sometimes waived**: Some banks don't charge receiving fees
- **Often unexpected**: Not always disclosed to sender

#### 3. Intermediary/Correspondent Bank Fees
- **Range**: $10-$50+ per intermediary bank
- **Major hidden cost**: Often NOT disclosed upfront
- **Multiple banks**: Transfer may pass through 1-3 intermediary banks
- **Deducted from principal**: Fees taken from the transferred amount
- **Example**: Send $1,000, recipient receives $950 after intermediary fees

#### 4. Currency Conversion Fees
- **Markup on exchange rate**: 1-3% above mid-market rate
- **Hidden in rate**: Banks use less favorable exchange rates
- **Additional conversion fee**: Sometimes charged separately
- **Total impact**: Can cost $10s-$100s depending on amount

#### 5. Cross-Border Transaction Fees
- **Card payments**: 0.6%-1.4% of amount sent
- **Bank wires (SWIFT)**: $35-$50 per transaction
- **Percentage-based**: 1-3% of transaction amount typical

### Total Cost Example (Real-World Scenario)

**Sending $10,000 USD to EUR in Germany:**
- Sending bank fee: $45
- Receiving bank fee: $15
- Intermediary bank fees: $30 (1-2 banks)
- Currency conversion markup (2%): $200
- **Total cost: $290 (2.9% of transfer)**
- **Recipient receives: $9,710 equivalent**

### Transfer Speed
- **Standard international**: 1-5 business days
- **Same-day/expedited**: Additional $10-$30 fee
- **SWIFT processing**: Multiple bank hops slow the process

### Hidden Costs to Watch
1. **Unfavorable exchange rates**: Marked up 1-3% above mid-market
2. **Intermediary bank deductions**: Not disclosed upfront
3. **Fees deducted from principal**: Recipient gets less than expected
4. **Double fees**: Both sender and receiver charged

## Trade Finance Costs

### Letters of Credit (LC)
- **Issuance fee**: Typically 0.5%-2% of LC value
- **Amendment fees**: $75-$150 per amendment
- **Advising fees**: €105 ($115) for advising another bank's LC
- **Confirmation fees**: Additional 0.5%-1.5% if confirmed LC required

### Bank Guarantees
- **Issuance**: Similar to LC, 0.5%-2% of guarantee amount
- **Amendment**: $75-$150 per change
- **Standby LC**: Similar fee structure

### Documentary Collections
- **Processing fee**: $50-$200 per collection
- **Lower than LC**: But less security for parties

### Correspondent Banking Services
- **Account maintenance**: Annual fees for nostro/vostro accounts
- **Transaction fees**: Per-transaction charges for services
- **Passed to customer**: Usually added to final customer cost

### Compliance and Regulatory Costs
- **KYC/AML screening**: Built into transaction costs
- **Regulatory reporting**: Overhead passed to customers
- **Sanctions screening**: Additional processing costs

## Custody and Asset Management Fees

### Traditional Custodial Services
- **Basis points on AUM**: 0.02%-0.10% annually (2-10 bps)
- **Minimum fees**: Often $50,000-$500,000 annually for institutional
- **Transaction fees**: Additional per-transaction charges
- **Settlement fees**: Per-trade settlement costs

### Example: $5B Family Office Custody
- **0.05% custody fee**: $2.5M annually
- **Transaction costs**: Variable based on volume
- **Compliance overhead**: Additional reporting costs

## Key Findings Summary

### Cost Dimensions for Comparison

1. **Subscription/Access Fees**
   - Traditional banking: Account maintenance, relationship fees
   - Often bundled or minimum balance requirements

2. **Setup/Deployment Costs**
   - LC setup: 0.5%-2% of value
   - Account opening: Often free but requires minimums
   - Correspondent relationships: Significant overhead

3. **Per-Transaction Costs**
   - Wire transfers: $25-$50 sending + $10-$20 receiving + $10-$50 intermediary
   - Total: $45-$120 per international wire transfer
   - Plus 1-3% currency conversion markup

4. **Ongoing Maintenance**
   - Account fees: Monthly/annual charges
   - Custody fees: 2-10 bps on assets under management
   - Relationship minimums: Opportunity cost

5. **Compliance/Regulatory**
   - KYC/AML: Built into transaction costs
   - Reporting: Administrative overhead
   - Sanctions screening: Processing delays and costs

6. **Go-to-Market Costs**
   - Relationship building: Time and resources
   - Multi-bank relationships: Redundancy for global coverage
   - Documentation: Legal and administrative burden

### Market Context
- **Cross-border payments market**: Projected $320 trillion by 2032
- **SWIFT network**: Dominant but slow and costly
- **Intermediary banks**: Major source of hidden costs
- **Transparency issues**: Fees often not disclosed upfront
