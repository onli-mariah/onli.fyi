## Key Insight

The document is showing **total cost of ownership** including:
1. Initial development (wallet, storefront, exchange, ERP, database, orchestration)
2. Platform fees (Oracle/Amazon/IBM membership + nodes)
3. Transaction costs
4. Ongoing maintenance and operations

This is the correct methodology for comparing platforms - not just platform fees alone.
