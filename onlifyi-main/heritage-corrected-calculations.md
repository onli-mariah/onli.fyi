- **Asset Denomination**: $100,000 per HLC microcommodity

## Liquidity Scenario: $500M Needed

### Traditional Options
**Secondary Sale:**
- Discount (12.5%): $62,500,000
- Transaction fees (2.5%): $12,500,000
- **Total Cost: $75,000,000**

**NAV Loan (3-year term):**
- Interest (8% × 3 years): $120,000,000
- Origination fee (2%): $10,000,000
- Legal fees: $5,000,000
- **Total Cost: $135,000,000**

### Onli HLC Solution

**Number of tokens for $500M:**
```
$500,000,000 ÷ $100,000 = 5,000 tokens
```

**Costs:**
- Developer Access: $6,000 (one-time)
- Asset Management ($500M AUM): $25,000 (annual)
- Issuance (5,000 tokens × $0.01): $50 (one-time)
- **Total Year 1: $31,050**
- **Ongoing: $25,000/year**

### Savings Analysis
- vs. Secondary Sale: $74,968,950 (99.96% savings!)
- vs. NAV Loan: $134,968,950 (99.98% savings!)

## Key Insights

1. **Asset-based pricing makes Heritage even more attractive**
2. **Higher denomination ($100k) = fewer tokens = lower costs**
3. **Savings vs. traditional methods are 99.9%+**
4. **Onli costs are negligible compared to traditional liquidity solutions**
5. **The $100k denomination is appropriate for institutional investors**
