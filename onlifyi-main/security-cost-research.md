# Security Cost Research Findings

## Source: Softstack - "How Much Does a Smart Contract Audit Cost in 2025?"
**URL:** https://softstack.io/blog/how-much-does-a-smart-contract-audit-cost-in-2025/
**Date:** July 16, 2025

### Key Findings

**Blockchain Exploit Losses:**
- **$3.8 billion stolen in 2022** due to smart contract vulnerabilities
- **$2.2 billion stolen in 2024** (reduced but still significant)
- Risk remains high as Web3 adoption surges

### Smart Contract Audit Pricing (2025)

| Project Type | Complexity | Cost Range | Timeline |
|-------------|-----------|------------|----------|
| Basic ERC20/NFT Token | Low | $3,000-$8,000 | 2-5 days |
| DeFi Protocol (DEX, Lending, Yield) | High | $25,000-$80,000+ | 2-3 weeks |
| Cross-Chain Bridge | Very High | $50,000-$150,000+ | 3-5 weeks |
| Full Suite (20K+ lines of code) | Extreme | $80,000-$250,000+ | 4-8 weeks |

### Cost Factors

**What determines audit pricing:**
1. Codebase size and complexity
2. Project type (DeFi introduces more attack vectors)
3. External integrations (oracles, bridges, third-party contracts)
4. Timeline urgency (tight deadlines require dedicated teams)
5. Auditor experience and reputation (premium firms charge more)

### Why Audits Matter

**When an exploit hits, you lose:**
- User trust
- Total value locked (TVL)
- Institutional interest
- Future revenue

**Benefits of professional audits:**
- Prevents catastrophic exploits
- Increases investor and user confidence
- Accelerates listing and fundraising processes
- May lower insurance premiums

### Insurance Considerations

- Some Web3 insurance providers offer **discounted premiums** for audited projects
- Most protocols still lack dedicated coverage
- Professional audit becomes **primary defense layer**

## Total Security Costs for Blockchain Marketplace

### Initial Security Investment

**Smart Contract Development & Audit:**
- Smart contract development: $50,000-$150,000
- Initial audit (DeFi-level complexity): $25,000-$80,000
- Follow-up audit after fixes: $10,000-$30,000
- **Total initial:** $85,000-$260,000

**Infrastructure Security:**
- Penetration testing: $10,000-$30,000
- Security architecture review: $20,000-$50,000
- **Total initial:** $30,000-$80,000

**Total Initial Security Investment:** $115,000-$340,000

### Ongoing Annual Security Costs

**Audits & Testing:**
- Annual smart contract re-audit (after updates): $25,000-$80,000
- Annual penetration testing: $10,000-$30,000
- Bug bounty program: $50,000-$500,000
- **Subtotal:** $85,000-$610,000

**Insurance:**
- Cybersecurity insurance: $100,000-$1,000,000
- **Subtotal:** $100,000-$1,000,000

**Monitoring & Response:**
- Security monitoring tools: $20,000-$80,000
- Incident response team/retainer: $50,000-$200,000
- **Subtotal:** $70,000-$280,000

**Total Annual Security Costs:** $255,000-$1,890,000

### 5-Year Security Costs for Blockchain Marketplace

- Initial: $115,000

- **Total:** $1,390,000
