# Niche Solutions Page Analysis

**Source:** https://mylogin09.wixstudio.com/niche/solutions
**Date Reviewed:** November 19, 2025

## Page Structure & Approach

### Hero Section
- Large hero image with overlay text
- Clear value proposition: "What if there was a new capital market where you can raise money without giving up equity"
- Single CTA: "Learn More"

### Content Organization
The page uses a **long-form scrolling narrative** with distinct sections:

1. **NICHE - A NEW KIND OF CAPITAL MARKET**
   - Explains what a Niche is (digital instantiation of legal agreement)
   - Key terms defined: contract price, face value, delivery date, maturity date
   - Emphasizes actual possession system vs. crypto
   - Legal framework: offering document with remedies, collateral, risks

2. **WHO FOR - IF ICO IS THE NEW STOCKS THEN NICHE IS THE NEW BONDS**
   - Split into two audiences:
     - **FOR THE INVESTOR:** Benefits (transparency, security, legal rights), avoiding crypto problems (no ledger, miners, forking, hacking)
     - **FOR THE ISSUER:** Capital raising without equity, pre-selling micro-commodities, debt-like contracts

3. **HOW DOES IT WORK - THINK ZERO COUPON BOND**
   - Simple analogy: works like zero coupon bonds
   - Face value issued at deep discount
   - Return = difference between face value and contract purchase price

4. **ELIA 5 - EXPLAIN IT LIKE I AM 5**
   - Step-by-step walkthrough with concrete example:
     - Become KYC member
     - Select offering (Species example)
     - Purchase contract: 5M Species @ $0.25 contract price ($1.25M total)
     - Face value: $1 per Species
     - Maturity: 12 months
     - Delivery: 24 hours before maturity
   - Two exit options:
     - Hold to maturity: $5M return (300% ROI)
     - Sell in secondary market: e.g., @ $0.35 after 30 days ($1.75M = 40% ROI)

5. **THE ONLI SOLUTION - A NICHE MARKET FOR CAPITAL**
   - Positioning: "THINK MICRO-COMMODITY (ONLI) NOT CRYPTO (BLOCKCHAIN)"

## Key Design Elements

### Visual Style
- Clean, modern design with 3D rendered graphics
- Red/coral accent color for key elements
- Large typography for section headers
- Video content embedded (boost01.mov)

### Content Strategy
- **Analogy-driven:** Zero coupon bonds, casino chips (from earlier context)
- **Problem-solution framework:** Crypto problems → Onli solutions
- **Concrete examples:** Species offering with specific numbers
- **Audience segmentation:** Clear investor vs. issuer benefits

### Narrative Flow
1. What it is (definition)
2. Who it's for (audience benefits)
3. How it works (mechanism)
4. Simple example (walkthrough)
5. Technology differentiation (Onli vs. blockchain)

## Comparison to Current Onli Use Cases Site

### Similarities
- Long-form content approach
- Clear value propositions
- Problem-solution framing

### Differences from Niche
1. **Niche uses single-page scrolling narrative** vs. our multi-page structure
2. **Niche has "ELIA 5" section** - very accessible concrete walkthrough
3. **Niche emphasizes legal framework** (offering documents, remedies, collateral)
4. **Niche uses financial analogies** (zero coupon bonds, ICO vs. bonds)
5. **Niche shows specific ROI examples** with numbers throughout

### Potential Improvements for Onli Use Cases
1. Add "ELIA 5" style walkthrough to each use case
2. Include more concrete ROI examples with specific numbers in hero sections
3. Consider single-page scrolling for overview/solutions page
4. Emphasize legal/compliance framework more explicitly
5. Use more financial analogies that business audiences understand

## Key Takeaways for Use Case Development

1. **Concrete examples matter:** Species offering shows exact numbers, timelines, ROI
2. **Multiple exit strategies:** Show flexibility (hold vs. sell early)
3. **Simplify complex concepts:** "Explain like I'm 5" makes it accessible
4. **Legal credibility:** Emphasize offering documents, remedies, compliance
5. **Audience-specific benefits:** Separate investor vs. issuer value props clearly
